If you ever need help, remember that there's a wiki for Clockwork:
https://github.com/red-stone-network/clockwork/wiki/User-Tutorials

If you want to learn how to get your own app working, there's a wiki for that too:
https://github.com/red-stone-network/clockwork/wiki/Dev-Tutorials
